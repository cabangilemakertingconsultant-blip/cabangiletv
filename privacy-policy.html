Cookie Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Cabangile TV uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website and streaming platform.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, remember preferences, improve user experience, and provide analytics and advertising services.

How We Use Cookies

Cabangile TV uses cookies for several purposes, including:

  • Keeping the website functioning correctly.
  • Remembering user preferences.
  • Improving website performance.
  • Understanding visitor behavior through analytics.
  • Supporting advertising and sponsored content.
  • Enhancing security and fraud prevention.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the operation of our website and services. Without them, certain features may not function properly.

2. Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our platform, allowing us to improve functionality and user experience.

3. Functional Cookies

These cookies remember settings such as language preferences and other customizations to improve your experience.

4. Advertising Cookies

Advertising partners, including Google, may use cookies to serve relevant advertisements based on your browsing activity and interests.

Google Advertising Cookies

Google and its partners may use cookies to personalize advertisements shown on Cabangile TV and across the web.

Google's advertising cookies enable it to serve ads based on your visits to our website and other websites.

You can manage your ad preferences through Google's advertising settings.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services that place cookies on our behalf, including:

  • Google Ads
  • Google Analytics
  • Supabase
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs)
  • Streaming and media services

These providers maintain their own privacy and cookie policies.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through browser settings. You may:

  • View stored cookies.
  • Delete cookies.
  • Block cookies.
  • Receive notifications before cookies are stored.

Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

POPIA Compliance

Cabangile TV is committed to complying with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). Any information collected through cookies is handled responsibly and used only for legitimate business purposes.
